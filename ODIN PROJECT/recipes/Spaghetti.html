Homemade Easy Pasta

Description.

Spaghetti is a long, thin, solid, cylindrical pasta. It is a staple food of traditional Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 piece of onion and minced- 5 cloves garlic and minced
  • 1/4 kg of carrots
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil/ olive
  • 6 pieces of hotdog (slice)
  • 1/4 grated cheese (110 grms.)
  • 1 can of evaporated and condensed milk
  • 900ml of spaghetti tomato sauce
  • 2 tsp of salt
  • 1/4 kg of ground pork

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  3. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surface and knead for 10 minutes. Let dough rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
  4. Divide dough into 8 balls; use a pasta machine to roll and cut dough into desired pasta shape.
